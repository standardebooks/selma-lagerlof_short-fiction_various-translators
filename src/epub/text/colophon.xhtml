Colophon

Short Fiction
was compiled from short stories published between 1894 and 1908 by
Selma Lagerlöf.
They were translated from Swedish between 1899 and 1910 by
Pauline Bancroft Flach, Jessie Brochner, and Velma Swanston Howard.

This ebook was produced for
Standard Ebooks
by
Robin Whittleton,
and is based on transcriptions produced in 2004 by
Nicole Apostola, Fay Dunn, Shaun Pinder, Roger Frank, and The Online Distributed Proofreading Team
for
Project Gutenberg
and on digital scans from the
Internet Archive.

The cover page is adapted from
Selma Lagerlöf,
a painting completed in 1908 by
Carl Larsson.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
